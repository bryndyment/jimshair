This week we visited the world renowned Hotel Saga. The semi formal dinner, hosted for all the advertising agencies in Iceland, was definitely a gala event. Everyone was dressed to the hilt. Even Linda Evens flew in for the occasion.

By the time they served dinner I thought I was going to faint. It was a 3 course meal the main corse consisting of lamb and ox tongue, um... yummy. It had a very interesting texture, sort of bubbly or foamy, kind of like a... cooked sponge.
