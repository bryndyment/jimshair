Here are a couple of food items alive and well in Iceland
that I thought had disappeared with my Schwinn Scrambler...



McDonald's styrofoam clamshell burger box

I thought I'd never again experience the convenience of popping open the lid to my Big Mac (or Filet-o-fish or McSviða, whatever), and pouring my fries into the top half of the container. Ah the little pleasures. I always wondered what McDonald's did with the excess boxes when they deemed them eco-mean and phased them out in the U.S..

TAB

The aftertaste alone will ensure you'll never forget TAB. A flavor that is best described as, well, TABBY. I don't know that it's unavailable in the states, but I sure haven't seen it in a while, especially in nice bottles like these.
