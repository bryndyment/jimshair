5 Minute Costume Solution


Plastic Man
Here is one that I saw last year that I thought was ingenious. Scary Plastic Man! All you need is a roll of saran wrap.

Directions
Take one roll of saran wrap. Rap it around your body until completely covered. Leave opening for mouth.

Caution:Stay away from open flames.

The great thing about this one is that you can reuse it.
