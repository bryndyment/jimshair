I don't know how tall it is, 15 stories? 20?
Anyway, you can see it from the Gottfólk roof.
On a clear, cold day, we decided to pay it a visit.







Jacqueline attempted the climb, but without gloves, the skin was ripped from the palms of her hands by the cold re-bar rungs, and she was forced to wait on the ground. Another setback was that the digital camera we brought only takes 8 shots. I climbed all the way to the top to find a cozy penthouse with a glorious view of the setting sun. I sat with its sole inhabitant, Mr. James Hoffa for about 3 hours eating banana pudding and playing his GameBoy. Too bad I was out of pictures.
