Food of the Week: Síld

This week we are featuring Síld. Some of you may already be familiar with this delightful treat. Perhaps you know them as rollmops or more commonly, pickled herring. Here in Iceland it is very common and comes in many different flavours. Such as curry, port, onion, tomatoe, confectionary, and many others. My particular favorite is the regular sea water flavour. Salty but sweet.
